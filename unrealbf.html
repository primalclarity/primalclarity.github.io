Unreal Battlefront

The Necris are advancing beneath an angry nano-black cloud, a fanatical menace intent on devouring all. Each victory brings them one step closer to prying the Sprawl away from the Imperium’s grasp – there the jihad will end, for the Necris believe once they silence the noise of humanity, they will bring forth the meta-consciousness that whispers to them across oceans of eternity of their great destiny to cleanse the universe.

Yet zeal breeds defiance, and the sons of Earth, the memory of the Skaarj invasion still fresh in their hearts, stand accused of denying a truth that all free men believe is their birth right. The Imperium proclaims Life is the Only Law; that they will never bow before an undead host intent on washing them away like a sin.

And so it begins – war, great and terrible, for the soul of men everywhere. The fate of mankind now lies in your hands – choose your destiny!

  • Experience a new gametype for Unreal Tournament 3 – epic warfare on open ended battlefronts, featuring unique objectives, troop reinforcements, a class selection system, and all the weapons and vehicles of the Unreal Universe.

  • Relive major battles in the epic war to protect mankind from the Necris Jihad.

    • Claim heathen worlds for your Necris brethren with no mercy.

    • Attack the Necris on their homeworld of Omicron 6 using surgical strikes.

    • Deploy robot armies against the Necris onslaught and cripple their momentum.

  • Each level offers unique playable units themed to each battleground. Designers can create their own unit types, and equip them however they like.

    • Play as Necris Heavy Troopers, and then as Liandri warriors, or as a Human Commando.
